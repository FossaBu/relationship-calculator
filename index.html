Relationship Status Calculator

Calculation Methodology

Weighted Factors

This score combines research-backed indicators of relationship commitment:

  • Time Together (20% weight): Based on Reis et al. (2017) showing shared time correlates with commitment.
  • Emotional Intimacy (25% weight): Highest weight per Sternberg's Triangular Theory of Love.
  • Future Plans (20% weight): Rusbult's Investment Model shows future orientation predicts stability.
  • Social Integration (15% weight): Lehmiller's research on "social embedding."
  • Sex Frequency (15% weight): Diminishing returns after 2x/week (Dean et al., 2014).

Non-Linear Relationships

  • Texting: Bell curve peaking at 20/day (too few = disinterest, too many = anxiety).
  • Sex: Normalized to 0-4x/week (more doesn't linearly increase commitment).
  • Conflict Resolution: Penalty only applies below 5/10 (Gottman's "Four Horsemen" theory).

Exclusivity Multiplier

1.3x boost based on Olmstead et al. (2018) showing exclusivity increases commitment by ~30%.

Key Sources: Sternberg (1986), Rusbult (1980), Gottman (1993), Lehmiller (2015)
